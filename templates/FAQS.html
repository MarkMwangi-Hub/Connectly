FAQs

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

How do I create an account on Connectly?

To create an account on Connectly, simply click on the “Sign Up” button on the homepage, provide your email, choose a username, and create a strong password. After that, you’ll be able to personalize your profile and start connecting with friends.

How can I manage my privacy settings?

You can easily manage your privacy settings from your account settings page. Here, you can choose who can see your posts, who can send you messages, and who can follow you. You can customize these settings to control your privacy.

How do I post content on Connectly?

Posting content on Connectly is simple! Just go to your feed, click on the “Create Post” button, add your text, images, or videos, and then hit “Post.” Your followers and friends will be able to engage with your content.

How can I find friends or other users?

To find friends or other users, click on the search bar at the top of the page. You can search by name, username, or interests. You can also filter your search results to find people you might know or be interested in following.

How can I delete my Connectly account?

If you wish to delete your account, go to your account settings and select the “Delete Account” option. Please note that once your account is deleted, all your data and posts will be permanently erased.

How do I block or report a user?

To block or report a user, visit their profile, click on the three dots (settings menu), and select either “Block” or “Report.” If you report a user, our moderation team will review the report and take necessary action.

How can I manage my notifications?

You can manage your notification preferences by going to your account settings and selecting “Notifications.” Here, you can choose whether to receive notifications for likes, comments, messages, and more.

How does Connectly handle content moderation?

Connectly employs automated and human moderation tools to ensure that all content posted complies with our community guidelines. Users can report inappropriate content, which will be reviewed by our moderation team.

How can I send messages to my friends?

To send a message, click on the "Messages" icon in the top right corner, choose the friend you want to message, and start typing your message. You can also send images, videos, and voice messages within your conversations.
